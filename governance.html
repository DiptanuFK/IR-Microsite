Environmental sustainability

Flipkart's sustainability approach is anchored in building a responsible, future-ready commerce ecosystem, where scale is matched with circularity, clean energy, and technology-led efficiencies across packaging, logistics, water, and waste.

Commitment to a phased integration of electric vehicles into the entire fleet, as part of the EV100 initiative
Scaled last-mile EV fleet to over 20,000 vehicles during 2025 festive, doubling year-on-year growth.

Diverted over 21,000 tons of waste from landfills across 12 TRUE-certified sites.

Launched the industry's first large-scale circular initiative, recycling 26,000 tons of paper and boosting overall scrap collection by 50%.
Recycled and reused 67M+ litres of wastewater annually, reducing freshwater dependence

Flipkart Foundation

Flipkart Foundation, the philanthropic arm of the Flipkart Group was formally launched in April 2022, reaffirming the Group's commitment to inclusive and equitable nation-building with the objective of "Building India Together."

Since 2022, the Flipkart Foundation has partnered with 21 organizations to support 35 projects across 17 states, impacting nearly 4.14 million underserved and marginalised individuals - over half of whom are women.

Over the years, the Flipkart Foundation has worked across multiple states, including Delhi, Haryana, Punjab, Uttar Pradesh, Assam, West Bengal, Odisha, Gujarat, Maharashtra, Telangana, Karnataka, and Andhra Pradesh, to support underserved communities through interventions in livelihoods, environment and community development, ensuring scalable and sustainable impact.

Since launch, 'Sahyog', the Foundation's employee volunteering initiative has engaged over 4,300 volunteers across the Flipkart Group, dedicating more than 6,000 volunteer hours and touching over 40,000 lives.

The Flipkart Foundation's journey has been recognized with 6 awards including the prestigious Mahatma Award for Social Good and Impact, encouraging us to keep pushing boundaries

I. Integrity as a Core Value, Not Just a Policy

Embedded in DNA

At Flipkart, Integrity is one of our five Core Values. It sits alongside Audacity and Customer First, ensuring that how we achieve results is just as important as the results themselves.

Leadership Commitment

From our Leadership to the last link in our Supply Chain, the message is clear: the long-term success of Flipkart depends entirely on the culture of integrity we set today.

The 'Right Way' Culture

We have fostered an environment where every employee is empowered to innovate with courage and can report concerns without fear of retaliation, therefore leading with uncompromising integrity.

Our Integrity Index score of 94%

proves that ethical conduct is woven into our daily operations. Consistent integrity index >90% over the last 4 years.

II. Strategic Business Integration (Compliance by Design)

Operational Excellence

We have successfully integrated compliance into our business growth. A prime example is the launch of 800+ Flipkart Minutes facilities with 100% license and safety adherence from day one.

Licensing Maturity

We've seen a transformation in our 'Licenses & Permits' program, moving from 20% to 99.9% valid licenses - a testament to our commitment to regulatory readiness at scale and gives us a competitive advantage.

Though we have 700+ TPIs across the group, we have set stringent Anti-Corruption compliance processes and due diligence.

III. Measurable Impact and Ecosystem Awareness

Seller Ecosystem

We don't just police; we educate. By consistently training over 175,000 sellers, we've seen an 80% drop in seller suspensions, creating a more stable and compliant marketplace.

IV. Recognition and the 'Human Factor'

Integrity in Action (IAA)

We celebrate those who go above and beyond through Walmart's Annual Global Recognition program. We select, recognise, and celebrate those who have demonstrated courage by choosing ethics over ease.

V. Our Forward Vision

Value-Driven Partnership

Our vision is to drive the execution of compliance processes while reducing risk and remaining a valued business partner. We are not a hurdle to the business; we are the foundation that allows it to run faster and safer.
